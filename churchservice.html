Missionary Service

The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

September 2023 - April 2025

Birmingham Alabama Mission

Served a full-time mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, beginning in the Birmingham Alabama Mission as a proselyting missionary, where I taught the gospel, worked closely with individuals and families, and developed strong communication, teaching, and service skills.

Salt Lake City South Mission

Later served in the Salt Lake City South Mission, supporting several Church departments in operational, administrative, and service-focused roles.

  • Materials Management / Retail Operations Team: supported distribution store expansion efforts and helped coordinate supplies and operational needs.
  • Publishing Services Department: served as a management assistant helping oversee and support 90+ missionaries.
  • Priesthood and Family Department: led FSY phone support for North America, assisting with communication and service coordination.
  • Global Services: served on the Church's web feedback team, helping respond to and support online user needs.
